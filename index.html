Osaka Aquarium Kaiyukan will reopen on June 1


We believe that serious measures are needed to prevent further spread of coronavirus infection.

We have implemented some practices to welcome our visitors.

Thank you for your understanding and cooperation.



Our safety measures



1.Online pre-booking is recommended to get your web-only ticket with a designated date and time.
- To maintain an appropriate distance in the aquarium
- To avoid waiting in a long line for getting tickets



2.We will ventilate through the building as possible for your safety.



3.Before entering the aquarium, all visitors are required to check the body temperature with a thermography camera.
Visitors with a body temperature of exceeding 37.5 degrees Celsiu are not allowed to enter.



Notes for visitors

- Please stay at home if you are not feeling well.
- Please wear a mask.
- Please keep an appropriate distance.
- Please do not speak loudly.
- Please do not touch the acrylic panels of exhibits or tanks.

Osaka Aquarium KAIYUKAN voted the
Japan's and Asia's Best in TripAdvisor
Travelers' Choice Awards 2014



Osaka Aquarium KAIYUKAN was selected as Japan's and
Asia's best aquarium by TripAdvisor®, the largest travel site in
the world. In the TripAdvisor Travelers' Choice™ Awards 2014
for Zoos and Aquariums, which are given to top zoos and
aquariums worldwide based on reviews and opinions posted by
TripAdvisor travelers, the KAIYUKAN ranked first in both
Japan's and Asia's top aquarium categories. It is a great honor
for the aquarium to receive high recognition by visitors from
both Japan and abroad. Osaka Aquarium KAIYUKAN strives to
make continued efforts to convey beautiful natural
environments created by precious marine life to visitors.



Osaka Free Wifi can be used around the Kaiyukan.
